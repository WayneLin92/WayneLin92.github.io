Weinan Lin (林伟南)

About Me

I am currently a Tenure-track Young Investigator in Shanghai Center for Mathematics Science, Fudan University.

My current research interests include stable homotopy theory and computational algebra for algebraic topology.

My PhD advisor is Prof. J. Peter May.
